History

In 2008, a great woman started to build the website of her dreams, a site where everybody can visit and send in pictures of their cats wearing hats. After the website was finished, the fabulous woman desided it would be a great idea to host a competition where you could wote for your favorite cat in a hat.

2011, the website won the official everybodylovescatwebpages award.
Don't miss out on our christmas special, spoiler - there will be cats with christmas hats.
